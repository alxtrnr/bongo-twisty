[Ridiculous](https://burjdubaiskyscraper.com) [skyscrapers](http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/americas/7472559.stm), grotesque [man made islands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Palm_Islands), [seven star hotels](https://www.uniqhotels.com/burj-al-arab/), [ski slopes in the desert](https://www.theguardian.com/travel/2005/nov/20/unitedarabemirates.wintersportsholidays.wintersports#send-share), extravagant displays of fortune, [abuse of human rights](https://www.hrw.org/sitesearch?search=dubai) and the exploitation of [resources](https://word-a-day-uae.blogspot.com/2006/08/counterpoint.html).

A country that specialises in unashamed displays of wealth, exclusion and privilege, a country that celebrates and gorges on the fat of irresponsible [pandering to the wealthy](http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/world/7740887.stm "BBC News"), to me Dubai is one of the most obscene countries in the world.

It strikes me as incredibly arrogant that this country which so gleefully embraces the mega rich imposes [acceptable standards of public behaviour](https://hajjency.com/what-are-the-public-behaviour-rules-in-dubai/) on it's visitors that require for example there to be [no canoodling on the beach](https://edition.cnn.com/2008/WORLD/meast/10/16/dubai.sex.couple.prison/index.html#cnnSTCText), bare chested strolls down the promenade, topless sunbathing, drinking in public and certainly [no drugs](https://www.independent.co.uk/news/world/middle-east/dubai-tourists-warned-over-tough-drug-stance-779910.html).

Sure you could argue that visitors should adhere to the local norms but it's difficult to see how the government there can be pious about anything given the value it places in material wealth over all else.

I know I'm not considering the whole story. Sure there is probably [lots of philanthropy](https://www.philanthropy.com/news/dubai-philanthropy-gives-money-to-american-charity/) from those who have the money to soothe their concious but all the same I just can't help feeling disgusted by the place and the people flocking there to spend their mega bucks.
