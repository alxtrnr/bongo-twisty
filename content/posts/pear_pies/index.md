Out on a ride over the weekend. The second for my ongoing RRtY effort. I prepared some tasty little pear pies to help fuel the ride. Had some gluten free dough left over from some pies I made last week to share with colleages at work. The dough was all the better for having been sitting in the fridge for a week. Gluten free dough makes the pies a bit softer when baked. Sweet and moist.
