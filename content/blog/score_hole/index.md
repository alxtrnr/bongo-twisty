Sadly for one reason or another there was not a sound to be heard from it when we were there. Thought that might have been because all the rocks, sticks and other junk that had been thrown into the horn. Turns out it may have been a lack of water. In times of drought – or plumbing problems – it is still possible to hear music, you just have to bring your own water.
