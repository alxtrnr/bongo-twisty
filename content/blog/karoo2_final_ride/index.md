It came off again on this mornings ride. My fault. I was still using the crappy ill fitting aftermarket bracket. I'd bodged it a bit to make it hold on tighter. That worked for the last week. It did not work this morning. The Karoo bounced out as I was going down a bumpy lane. Noticed straight away this time. Stopped and retrieved it. The screen would no longer display. It saved the ride till that point though and synced it when I got home. Tracked the rest of the ride on my phone. Joined the two together on RWGPS and voila! My ride was recorded.
